## 验证结果

### ✅ 数据库验证

```sql
-- 白云区教师
SELECT s.name, u.real_name, t.subjects
FROM schools s
JOIN teachers t ON s.id = t.school_id
JOIN users u ON t.user_id = u.id
WHERE s.district = '白云区' AND s.id IN (15, 16);

-- 结果:
-- 白云区第一小学 | 陈刚 | {数学}
-- 白云区第一中学 | 蒋敏 | {信息科技}

-- 白云区管理员
SELECT u.username, u.real_name, d.name, ap.permission_scope->>'review_subjects'
FROM users u
JOIN admin_permissions ap ON u.id = ap.user_id
JOIN districts d ON ap.district_id = d.id
WHERE u.username = 'baiyun_admin';

-- 结果:
-- baiyun_admin | 白云区教育局管理员 | 白云区 | ["数学", "信息科技"]

-- 题目统计
SELECT subject, grade, COUNT(*) as total
FROM question_bank
WHERE subject IN ('数学', '信息科技')
  AND created_by IN (58, 60)
  AND status = 'published'
GROUP BY subject, grade;

-- 结果: 16行记录（数学9个年级 + 信息科技7个年级）
```
